About RetinaAI

Thoughtful AI for retinal screening

RetinaAI applies deep learning to retinal fundus photography to help present diabetic-retinopathy screening information clearly and responsibly.

How it works

1

Upload a clear retinal fundus image.

2

Analyze visual retinal features with an EfficientNet-based model.

3

Review a clear probability-based report with an appropriate next step.

Important clinical note

RetinaAI is intended as a decision-support tool. It is not a medical device, does not replace professional diagnosis, and should not be used as the sole basis for treatment decisions. Always consult a qualified eye-care clinician.
